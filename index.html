Rural Economic Development Activities Trust

REDAT (Rural Economic Development Activities Trust) is a service-minded voluntary and non-governmental organization established in 2007 and registered in the sub-registrar office in Ulundurpet Taluk, villupuram district with Reg. No: 4506/2007 under the Indian Trust Act 1882. Our mission is to address ignorance, illiteracy, and exploitation, which are major hindrances for socially deprived and disadvantaged people, and to improve their standard of life through various support programs.

We strive to empower women and children by providing education, vocational training, and access to healthcare. By fostering a sense of community and self-reliance, we aim to create sustainable development and long-term social change. Our holistic approach ensures that all aspects of an individual's well-being are addressed, promoting a more inclusive and equitable society. Join us in our mission to uplift the underprivileged and make a lasting impact on their lives.

Vision

"To improve the livelihood of poor and downtrodden peoples towards their upliftment, educationally, economically, and socially through an integrated participatory development approach."

Our Programmes

Women Development

Our Women Development program focuses on enhancing the social and economic status of women. We offer workshops and mentorship opportunities that equip women with essential skills for personal and professional growth.

Skill Development Workshops: Hands-on training in areas like handicrafts, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy.

Mentorship Programs Connecting women with experienced professionals for guidance and support.

Health

Access to health care is vital for women’s empowerment. Our health initiatives aim to improve physical and mental well-being.

Health Awareness Campaigns: Informative sessions on reproductive health, nutrition, and mental wellness.

Health Screenings: Regular health check-ups and screenings for early detection of health issues.

Education

We believe that education is a powerful tool for change. Our educational programs target various age groups to ensure lifelong learning.

Literacy Programs: Classes designed for women of all ages to improve reading and writing skills.

Scholarship Opportunities: Financial support for women pursuing higher education.

Awareness Programs

Awareness is key to empowerment. We conduct community outreach initiatives to educate women and families about their rights and available resources.

Workshops on Women’s Rights: Sessions that inform women about legal rights and social services.

Community Awareness Events: Engaging activities to spread knowledge about health, education, and empowerment.

Self-Training

Empowerment starts with self-reliance. Our self-training programs encourage women to develop skills that foster independence.

Self-Help Groups: Providing a platform for women to share experiences, support each other, and learn new skills.

Online Training Modules: Flexible courses available online for women to learn at their own pace.
